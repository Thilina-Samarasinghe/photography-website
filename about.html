About Malcolm Lismore

Malcolm Lismore is a freelance photographer based on the North West coast of Scotland. With a passion for the natural world, Malcolm captures the rugged Scottish landscape, its natural wildlife, and coastal birds. He also offers photography services for weddings, portraits, and special events.

With years of experience and a keen eye for detail, Malcolm's work has been featured in numerous publications and exhibitions. His unique style and dedication to his craft make him a sought-after photographer in the region.
